More precisely: define the layer of a tree vertex as the length of the path from it to its leftmost leaf. The layer of a leaf is 0, the layer of a vertex whose left child is a leaf is 1, and so on. Assume that chunk_size≥2⋅width. Then, the light verifiable stream of a string is its baseline verifiable stream, except omitting those labels that are contributed to the stream as left children and that are not labelling vertices whose layer is divisible by ⌈chunk_size/width⌉.
