For the client to tell the server which parts of the verification metadata need not be included in a slice stream because the client already has that data, it hence suffices for the client to supply two integers between 0 and 64: the left_skip indicates to omit from the stream the labels of the children of the first left_skip vertices on the path from the root to the first chunk of the slice, and the right_skip indicates to omit from the stream the labels of the children of the first right_skip vertices on the path from the root to the final chunk of the slice.
